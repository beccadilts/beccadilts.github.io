Lirica ‘Stralia

December 2017

Objectives

Create a poster for a fictional opera festival called “Lirica ‘Stralia.” The poster should let the image speak for itself, with the information about the event supporting the image.

Target

  • Opera goers
  • Tourists visiting Australia

Concept

The poster idea involved Australia as the event’s location and an opera festival as the event in mind. To create a recognizable image, I took common symbols of Australia and of the opera, the koala and opera glasses respectively, to describe the event. To further bring out the unique aspects of Australia, I used an Australian Indigenous approach with dot painting and red, earthen colours. While the koala is the main focus there is also a subtle pattern in the back with these dots and boomerangs.
